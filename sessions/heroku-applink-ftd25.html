French Touch Dreamin'

Scale your Salesforce apps and skills with Heroku AppLink

The Salesforce platform now has a new outlet that lets you build practically anything while staying within Salesforce. Heroku AppLink is a new, free add-on for Heroku that integrates code in practically any language of your choice, deployed to Heroku, seamlessly and securely into your Apex, Flow, and Agentforce-based experiences. In this session, you will gain a basic understanding of Heroku, learn how to scale elastically beyond traditional Apex limits, and see how to deploy code that integrates natively with Salesforce. You will also take away sample code and the skills to deploy your own Heroku sidecar services attached to your orgs.
